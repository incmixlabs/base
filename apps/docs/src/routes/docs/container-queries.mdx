  </Heading>
  <div className="space-y-3">
    <Text size="md">
      <strong>Better proportions out of the box.</strong> Padding, spacing, and internal rhythm can adapt to the space
      a component actually receives.
    </Text>
    <Text size="md">
      <strong>Fewer layout-specific overrides.</strong> Components can stay balanced in narrow and wide contexts
      without special-case styling everywhere they are reused.
    </Text>
    <Text size="md">
      <strong>More reusable surfaces.</strong> Cards, containers, and form surfaces behave more like self-contained UI
      elements and less like viewport-dependent fragments.

  </div>
